About

Goa is known for its striking landscape, famous beach in India, astounding monuments and churches and bustling Goa nightlife. Goa is one of the most visit tourist places in India,especially on weekends, as it is not very far from mumbai and having Panaji as its capital and Vasco da Gama as the largest city. It attracts a large number of tourists from all over the world,round the year.
